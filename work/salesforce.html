Case study — Salesforce, Industries Studios

Copy across 14 different industries, one voice.

From utilities to telecom, every Salesforce industry has its own regulations, buyers, and jargon. The job was to write inside all of them, on deadline, on-brand, every time.

Copywriter Enterprise Multi-Industry

Role

Copywriter · 14 industries · Industries Studios team

I was one of several copywriters for the Industries Studios team, crafting and editing copy across 14 different industries.

I worked within tight and varied timelines, collaborating directly with partners to ensure brand and industry guidelines were met on every piece, from campaign emails to webinar promotion.

Utilities: making the case for customer experience to an industry used to being the only option.

Telecom: pitching network-as-a-service to an audience that's heard every buzzword twice.

Fourteen industries. Fourteen sets of rules. One consistent voice.
